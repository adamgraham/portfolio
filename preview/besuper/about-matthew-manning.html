Matthew Manning

Written by Krystal Kara

 

Matthew Manning is one of my closest friends and is someone who has more compassion for people in his pinky than most do in their whole body. Matthew wears his heart on his sleeve and isn’t afraid for people to see the amount of love for others pulsing through his veins. He constantly humbles me. He is one of those humans who are willing to give up everything for others. His passion is to educate others on inequalities in society through selflessness, love, compassion and stories.

 

Without Matthew, Be Super wouldn’t exist. Matthew has an uncanny way to help individuals realize their full potential and will do whatever it takes to help them achieve their dreams. There is no one else better suited to work on Be Super than Matthew simply put he is super. Matthew is one of my personal heroes. He is right up there with Batman, Kamala Khan, and Deadpool. I think he secretly goes out at night and uses his superpower of getting people to connect with their compassion; that would partially explain why everyone around him are helping to change the world for the better. Whenever he is around you can feel the energy shift and the love seep through the atmosphere. Matthew exemplifies everything that Be Super is about.
