Committee

DemonTHON's committee works year round to create and foster a truly inspirational event that honors the patients and families of Lurie Children's Hospital. It is a commitment to something greater than our individual experiences at DePaul University. Committee applications open shortly after the Big Event every year - we strongly encourage you to apply!
