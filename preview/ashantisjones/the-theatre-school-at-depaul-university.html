The Theatre School.

Assistant Box Office Manager, Winter 2015

The Theatre School at DePaul University is one of America's oldest and most respected theatre conservatories. Our highly specialized programs employ a learning-by-doing approach, featuring student work in all areas of production in more than 40 plays each season.

 

As apart of course study, Ashantis held the position of Assistant Box Office Manager for The Theatre School at DePaul University. In this position she facilitated scheduling for the student workers, ran weekly/end of run reports for productions, and gained basic knowledge in the Choice ticketing system.
