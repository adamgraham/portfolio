Events

DemonTHON hosts events on campus and throughout the City of Chicago that engage students at DePaul University and families that are being treated at Ann & Robert H. Lurie Children's Hospital of Chicago. Attending any of our events is the easiest way to get involved, meet new people, and have fun! If you have any questions about any of our events, email our Special Events Director at demonthon.specialevents@gmail.com.

The Big Event

The Big Event is a 24-hour celebration of the tremendous fundraising efforts of DePaul students and an amazing opportunity for all to connect with Miracle Families from the Chicagoland area that are being treated at Lurie Children’s Hospital. During the Big Event dancers commit to standing for the full 24-hour duration of the event, dancing every hour on the hour. Dancers stand to be in solidarity with kids and families who battle pediatric illness every day. The Big Event is not only a celebration of the dancers, but more importantly, the strength and courage of families from Lurie Children's.

 

Date: April 29-30th, 2016

Time: 5:00 PM - 5:00 PM (24-hours FTK!)

Location: McGrath Arena

 

 

 

Miracles 'til Midnight

Miracles ‘til Midnight is DemonTHON’s , 24-hour fundraising initiative. It is designed to draw attention to DemonTHON throughout the DePaul community, motivate dancers to fundraise, and engage the entire city of Chicago in DemonTHON’s fundraising efforts. Miracles ‘til Midnight is DemonTHON’s second-largest fundraising event of the year. This year we are trying to raise $48,000 in 24 hours!

 

Date: March 3rd, 2016

Time: 12:01 AM- 11:59 PM (24-hours FTK!)

Location: DePaul Campus

 

 

 

For The Kup (Competition)

For The Kup is DemonTHON's annual competition in which teams compete to earn spirit points in order to win the Miracle Kup at the end of year! The winning team will be recognized at the Big Event, receive perks and prizes throughout the 24 hours, and have the glory of being the Kup winners until next year’s big event. Spirit points are given to the teams who show the most dedication, support, and enthusiasm throughout the year.
