Dancers

Our dancers are the core of the DemonTHON experience. Registering as a dancer for our Big Event is an experience you truly won't forget. After 24 hours of being on your feet and listening to all of the inspirational stories of how Lurie Children’s hospital has helped all of our miracle families, you will look back at all of your hard work and know that it was worth it! Below are the steps to become a dancer and attend the Big Event.

1. Find a Dancer Representative for your organization or team

The dancer representative is the captain of your team and takes on a little bit of extra responsibility for the team. If you need help finding a team to join, or if you would like to become a dancer rep contact our Recruitment Director at demonthon.recruitment@gmail.com.

 

2. Sign-Up on DonorDrive

In order to attend the event, you must sign-up under our event on DonorDrive.

Register for DemonTHON 2016

 

3. Fundraise For The Kids

Dancers have to fundraise a minimum of $250 in order to attend the Big Event. Fundraising the money is not as hard as it seems, and it is entirely worth it when you look back at all of your hard work and can say you stood For The Kids at DemonTHON! Check out our Fundraising Page for great tips on raising money. Even though registration is currently not open, the earlier you start fundraising the easier it will be. You can still start today!

 

4. Dance!

You've done it! DemonTHON here you come!
